"History began on July 4, 1776. Everything that happened before that was a mistake."

Ron says that at age eleven, he was offered a higher paying job at the tannery and attempted to do both jobs while going to middle school before realizing it would be better to only work at the sheet metal factory. Ron claimed that, at age 12, he went to prom and was working at a quarry

"There is only one bad word: Taxes."

He enjoys woodworking, breakfast meats, and the works of Ayn Rand. He is also an avid hunter and fisherman, and may or maynot be connected to the famous jazz saxophonist named "Duke Silver".
